### 1. Capital Gains Tax (NRI Rules 2025)
* **New Rule (Finance Act 2024/25):** NRIs typically pay a flat **12.5%** Long Term Capital Gains (LTCG) tax on property sold after July 23, 2024.
* **Indexation Note:** Unlike residents, NRIs usually **do not** get the benefit of "Indexation" (inflation adjustment) on the 2001 price.
* **The Formula:**
    * `Sale Price` (â‚¹2 Cr) MINUS `FMV 2001` (â‚¹25 Lakhs) = **Profit** (â‚¹1.75 Cr).

### 2. The "Lower TDS" Strategy (Form 13)
